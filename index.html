Why Pako Pako?

We chose Pako Pako as our signature gelato flavor because of its unique taste and incredible health benefits. Pako Pako, a type of edible fern, is a traditional Filipino ingredient known for its distinct earthy flavor that pairs perfectly with the creamy texture of gelato.

The Background of Pako Pako

"Pako" is a Filipino term for an edible fern, scientifically known as "Diplazium esculentum," which is commonly referred to as the "fiddlehead fern" in English; it is a native plant to the Philippines that grows abundantly near streams and in mountainous regions, often harvested for its young, coiled fronds which are used in various Filipino dishes, typically cooked in salads, sauteed with garlic and onion, or added to coconut milk-based stews (gata) due to its unique flavor and texture.
